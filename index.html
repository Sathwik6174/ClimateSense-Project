Causes of Climate Change

- what causes climate change in reality??

Fossil Fuels

Burning coal, oil, and gas produces carbon dioxide and nitrous oxide.

Deforestation

Cutting down forests means trees can no longer absorb CO2 from the atmosphere.

Agriculture

Livestock like cows and sheep produce large amounts of methane when they digest food.

Climate Hero: Prof. Chetan Singh Solanki

Known as the "Solar Gandhi of India," Prof. Chetan Singh Solanki, an IIT Bombay professor and founder of the Energy Swaraj Foundation known for his pioneering work in solar energy and his 11-year "Energy Swaraj Yatra" (Journey for Energy Independence), traveling across India in a solar-powered bus to promote 100% solar adoption and raise climate change awareness, much like Gandhi's non-violent movements. He is also called the "Solar Man of India" for his efforts to create a public movement for sustainable energy. Key Aspects of His Work: Energy Swaraj Movement: Aims to achieve energy independence and sustainability by promoting the use of solar energy. Energy Swaraj Yatra (2020-2030): An ambitious 11-year journey on a solar bus to educate people and practice what he preaches, not returning home until the mission is complete. Climate Change Action: Focuses on practical solutions, urging individuals to reduce consumption and transition to solar power. Initiatives: Includes training rural communities, creating solar devices, and campaigns like "This election, think of a climate correction". Why "Solar Gandhi"? He draws inspiration from Mahatma Gandhi's ideals of self-reliance and sustainable living, applying them to the energy sector through education and grassroots movements, hence the nickname.

The Energy Swaraj Roadmap

Follow the AMG Principle by Prof. Chetan Singh Solanki to save the planet.

1

AVOID

Avoid the use of energy wherever possible. Switch off lights when leaving a room. Use natural light during the day.

2

MINIMIZE

If you cannot avoid, minimize. Use energy-efficient appliances (LEDs, 5-star rated ACs) and reduce your consumption by 1/3rd.

3

GENERATE

Only after avoiding and minimizing, generate your own energy locally using Solar Power. Become a 'Prosumer' (Producer + Consumer).
